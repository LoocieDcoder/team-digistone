Techlia Women Entrepreneurship Program (TWEP)

Techlia Women Entrepreneurship program (TWEP) is a program designed to inspire and encourage women who are willing and motivated with a lot of start up ideas but don't have the necessary mentorship and funding needed. TWEP matches them with primary/angel investors and mentors to get proper funding and structure which actually pushes the African economy to greater heights.

About Entrepreneurship

Techlia Women Entrepreneurs Programme (TWEP)

Women Entrepreneurs Program 6-month fellowship organized by TECHLIA for outstanding women entrepreneurs from emerging tech markets. It brings together female leaders from all over the world, who are united in the pursuit to achieve entrepreneurial greatness in tech. We offer targeted support to scale our founders' ventures and develop their leadership abilities, with the following program plan: Kick-Off Conference: A two-day digital kick-off conference will take place in the first week of the 6 months remote fellowship .

Remote Program: The Young Founders will receive six months of support including a personal mentor, monthly peer mentoring sessions, monthly expert workshops, and networking opportunities with corporates and investors. At Africa Leadership Conference every year The ten best preforming Female Founders will be invited to a three-day fully funded conference . During this conference, the founders will have the opportunity to pitch in front of investors, participate in workshops, join networking events and get insights into the African start-up scene.

Selection criteria

We are looking for female entrepreneurs with high potential for impact and growth to become leaders of a sustainable future, with the following criteria:

You have recently started a for-profit company focused on developing or emerging markets.

Your company possesses a scalable business model that has ideally generated revenue or secured the first round of external funding.

You or your business possesses disruptive qualities that can increase the quality, impact, or inclusiveness of the industry.

You possess a good working knowledge of English.

You are available to attend the digital kick-off conference and can make time to participate in program activities for six months.

Application Process

The following application will provide our Program Team with an understanding of your startup and profile. Please have the following key elements ready:
