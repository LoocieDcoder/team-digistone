About Us

Welcome to Techlia

We are a non-profit organization focused on celebrating and empowering women in tech globally.

Techlia was founded by a group of ambitious women who wants to power impact in the world.We wants to a smarter generation of female leaders in the tech and entrepreurship industry.

Research shows that only 26% of technology jobs are held by women. We are bridging that gap by equipping women to get started in and sustain Tech Careers and Entrepreneurship with relevant and on-demand tech and digital skills.
